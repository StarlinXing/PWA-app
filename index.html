How to Install

1

Tap the Share Button

On your mobile device, tap the share button in your browser.

2

Select "Add to Home Screen"

Look for the option to add the app to your home screen.

3

Tap "Add"

Confirm by tapping "Add" and the app will appear on your home screen.

Or Use the Install Button

If you see the install button in the address bar or below, simply tap it to install the app.
